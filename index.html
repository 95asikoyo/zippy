Our Services

Digital Printing

Our digital printing department handles business cards, fliers, posters, letterheads, wedding cards, CD/DVD branding, certificates, complimentary slips, eulogies, stickers, Job ID Cards and many more. We print any amount you may request.

Large Format Printing

Our Large Format Department handles everything from pull up banners, bill boards, vehicle graphics, window graphics, flags, stand alone banners, light boxes, pedestal display among many more

Point of Sale Department

Our Point Of Sale department handles all company merchandise including t shirts, mouse pads, mugs, pens, badges, lanyards, buntings, cards holders, glasses, umbrellas, caps, wrist bands, watches e.t.c

Signage

Our signage department offers 3D outdoor signs, LED Signage, informative signage e.g toilet indicators, Digital Scrolling Boards among many more. Stand out from the rest of the pack by getting one for your business

Binding Department

Our binding department offer all types of binding i.e perfect bonding, saddle stitching, wire binding, tape binding, screw binding, hardcover binding. We also offer cards cornering to achieve that elegant look.

Engraving and Direct Branding

The engraving and direct branding deals with diaries, pens, flash disks, power banks, water bottles, serviettes, wrist bands, pens e.t.c
